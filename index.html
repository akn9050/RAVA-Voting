Welcome to Rava Voting.

A cryptographically secure voting system for truly democratic elections. Are fair elections possible? Yes!

Introduction!

Democracy depends on the proper administration of popular elections. Voters should receive assurance that their intent was correctly captured and that all eligible votes were correctly tallied. The election system as a whole should ensure that voter coercion is unlikely, even when voters are willing to be influenced. These conflicting requirements present a significant challenge: how can voters receive enough assurance to trust the election result, but not so much that they can prove to a potential coercer how they voted? Rava is a system that solves these problems and provides cryptographically secure e-voting.

Status

Rava Voting is currently in active development, and it is not ready to be used yet.
